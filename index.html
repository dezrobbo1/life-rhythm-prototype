About Prototype 1.4.4

Prototype 1.4.4 keeps task timing and adds Start Boost: a behaviour-based task-start support layer for reducing friction, adding interest and learning what helps you begin. It does not use dopamine-hacking claims.

Not included yet: AI, cloud sync, accounts, notifications, calendar integration, partner sharing, streaks, badges, therapy-style check-ins or clinical advice.

Boundary: Life Rhythm is non-clinical self-management support. It does not diagnose or treat ADHD, provide therapy, prescribe food/exercise, manage crisis, or replace professional care.
